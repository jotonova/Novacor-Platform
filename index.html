Step-by-Step Guide
1

Start with Expense Breakdown

Always begin on the Expense Breakdown tab. Fill in Property Details at the top first — address, type, size, year, beds, baths, renovation level. These carry across every page automatically. Then enter renovation costs line by line. Totals flow into all evaluation pages automatically.

2

Choose Your Strategy Tab

Pick the tab that matches your investment plan: Flip Analysis to buy, renovate, and sell; BRRRR Method to refinance and rent after rehab; or Rental Analysis for a buy-and-hold.

3

Enter Key Financials Once

On the Flip Analysis tab, enter Target Purchase Price, ARV, and CMA. These auto-carry to BRRRR and Rental pages. For Selling/Closing Costs, use the % dropdown to auto-calculate or enter a dollar amount directly.

4

For Rentals & BRRRR: Add Income & Mortgage

Enter monthly rent, insurance, maintenance reserve, and other expenses on the Rental tab. Add loan amount, rate, and term to get your mortgage calculated automatically. These sync to the BRRRR page.

5

Read Your Results

All metrics calculate instantly. Green = hits target. Red = doesn't. Use progress bars to see how close you are. Adjust thresholds using the ⚙ Thresholds button.

6

Save & Load Deals

Use Save Deal on the Save/Load tab to store deals in your browser. Use Download Deal File to export a .json file you can reload at any time. Deals persist across sessions.
